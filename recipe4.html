Flourless banana cake - gluten free, lactose free

If you love a good banana cake this will surely hit the spot with plenty of that sweet banana flavour.

Health Info

Bananas contain large amounts of potassium and is therefore often recommended to people with high blood pressure that needs more potassium in their diets.

Ingredients

  • 500g mashed very ripe bananas
  • 325g almond butter
  • 75g maple syrup
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp. vanille powder
  • Â¼ tsp. sea salt
  • 150g dark chocolate

Steps

  1. Combine mashed bananas, almond butter, syrup, eggs, vanilla powder and sea salt and whisk together to a smooth batter.
  2. Chop the chocolate into smaller pieces and mix it into the batter.
  3. Transform the batter to a parchment paper lined baking dish (20 x 20 cm).
  4. Bake the cake in a heated oven at 320F for 90 minutes.
  5. Let the cake cool completely before cutting it into squares.

The Healthy Baker

We believe that knowledge is the best motivation for a healthy lifestyle.
