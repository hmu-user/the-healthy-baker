Blackberry muffins - gluten free, lactose free, nut free

Delicious muffins that will only take you a few minutes to make.

Health Info

Blackberries is an excellent source of vitamin C, which is good for the immunesystem.

Ingredients

  • 70g coconut flour
  • 70g desiccated coconut
  • 140g coconut syrup or maple syrup kokossirup
  • 140g coconut milk
  • 4 eggs
  • 70g mild flavoured olive oil
  • 1 tsp. vanilla powder
  • Â½ tsp. sea salt
  • 200g blackberries

Steps

  1. Combine coconut flour, desiccated coconut, vanilla powder and sea salt.
  2. Whisk togehter coconut syrup, coconut milk, olive oil and eggs.
  3. Mix the dry ingredients with the wet ingredients and combine well.
  4. Gently fold in the blackberries.
  5. Line up 12 muffin paper cups on a baking sheet.
  6. Grease the paper cups (optional) and fill them with batter.
  7. Place the buns on a parchment paper lined baking sheet.
  8. Bake the muffins in a heated oven at 320F for 40 minutes.
