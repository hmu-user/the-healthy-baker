Brownie with walnuts and 'milk' chocolate - gluten free

Dark,rich and oh so good - this brownie is pure chocolate heaven!

Health Info

Dark chocolate and unsweetened natural cocoa powder have many health benefits. Studies have shown positive results in mood improvement and in regards to cardiovascular health.

Ingredients

  • 250g dark chocolate (min. 70% but the darker the better)
  • 125g butter
  • 3 eggs
  • 200g coconut sugar
  • 250g almond flour
  • 25g cocoa powder
  • Â½ tsp. sea salt
  • 100g walnuts
  • 100g raw 'milk' chocolate

Steps

  1. Combine dark chocolate and clarified butter in a small pot and melt it slowly over very low heat.
  2. Whip eggs and coconut sugar together until light and frothy.
  3. Make sure that the melted chocolate and butter is well combined and mix it in with the eggs and sugar.
  4. Add almond flour, cocoapowder and sea salt and mix the batter thoroughly.
  5. Chop up the walnuts and the 'milk' chocolate and fold them in the batter.
  6. Line a 25 x 20 cm baking dish with parchment paper and transfer the batter to the dish.
  7. Bake the brownie in a heated oven at 320F for 40-45 minutes.
  8. Let the brownie cool completely before serving.

We believe that knowledge is the best motivation for a healthy lifestyle.
