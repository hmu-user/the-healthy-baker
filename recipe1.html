Coconut buns - gluten free, dairy free, nut free

Eat the buns fresh out the oven with butter or use them as part of your lunch. They go great with soups and stews as well.

Health Info

Coconut flour is a great nut free alternative, when baking grain free goods. The flour is naturally low in fat, while high in fibers and protein.

Ingredients

  • 100g coconut flour
  • 20g psyllium husk
  • Â½ tsp. sea salt
  • 5 eggs
  • 200g coconut milk
  • 50g mild olive oil
  • 1 tbsp. maple syrup

Steps

  1. Combine coconut flour, psyllium husk and sea salt.
  2. Whisk togehter eggs, coconut milk, olive oil and maple syrup.
  3. Mix the dry ingredients with the wet ingredients and combine well.
  4. Leave the dough to rest for a few minutes.
  5. Stir the dough well one last time.
  6. Divide the dough into six equal parts and shape them into round balls (if the dough is sticky, coat your hands with cold water).
  7. Place the buns on a parchment paper lined baking sheet.
  8. Bake the buns in a heated oven at 320F for 60 minutes.
