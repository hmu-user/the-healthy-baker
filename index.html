About Us

We are a small team of dedicated people focusing on bringing healthy baked goods to everyone.

Our goal is to provide people with healthy and allergy friendly baked goods recipes and inform them about the health benefints of the ingredients used. Since we started there has been great interest and we have received positive feedback from many people.

The Healthy Baker

The Healthy Baker is a recipe site with healthy baked goods. The site consist of easy to follow recipes using simple and nutritious ingredients. All the recipes are without gluten and some without lactose and nuts as well. The Healthy Baker helps you understand what health properties the food you eat have, which makes it easier for you to make healthier choices in your diet.

Mission

Our mission is to make it easy and delicious for you to eat healthy.! Finding out what's healthy and what's not can be quite tricky, so we decided to create this recipe app for you. Here you'll find health tweaked recipes of your favourite sweets and treats, while learning how natural and wholesome ingredients are good for you. We believe that knowledge is the best motivation for a healthy lifestyle.
